senior thesis

CRYSTALLINE - SPRING 01

Crystalline is the first collection by Esteem. This concept plays upon Esteem's brand idea of "polished femininity" by introducing soft and romantic colors and prints for a February delivery.
 


 
UNDERWATER - SPRING 02

Underwater is an extension of Esteem's previous collection "Crystalline." However, Underwater explores colors and prints that provide the Esteem woman with cooler tones. This concept delves into ultramarine trends that are significant to the intended April season.
